MORALS

What are morals?

Principles on which
one's judgments of right
and wrong are based.
These are influenced by society,
culture and religion.

ETHICS

What are ethics?

Guiding principles
of conduct of
an individual or group
They are influenced by
profession, field and
organization.

In Quran....

at many places Allah
has told us different ethics and
morals that we can follow and increase
our ranks in this world and
in Jannah.

#01

"And do not kill
anyone which Allah
has forbidden
except for a
just cause."
(Quran 17:33)

#02

"And donot approach
the property of an orphan
except in the way that is best
until he reaches maturity.
And fulfill [every] commitment. Indeed
the commitment is ever [that
about which one will be] questioned."
Quran(17:34)

#03

"And give full measure
when you measure, and weigh
with an even [i.e. honest] balance.
That is the best way
and best in result."
Quran(17:35)

#04

"O you who have believed
avoid much [negative] assumption
Indeed, some assumption is sin.
And donot spy or backbite eachother."
(Quran 49:12)

#05

"O you who have believed
let not a people ridicule people;
perhaps they may be better than
them; nor let women ridicule women; perhaps
they may be better than them. And donot insult
one another and donot call eachother by
nicknames."
Quran(49:11)

#06

"And your Lord has decreed
that you worship not except HIM, and
to parents, good treatment, Whether one or both of
them reach old age with you, say not to them,
"uff", and donot repel them but speak
to them a noble word."
Quran(17:23)
